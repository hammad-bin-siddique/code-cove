How this converter works

This tool pulls live exchange rates from an open, free exchange rate feed and calculates the conversion instantly in your browser. Rates are refreshed daily, which is accurate enough for everyday use, travel planning, or checking approximate freelance/invoice values across currencies.

For time sensitive, high value transactions, always confirm the exact rate with your bank or payment provider, since real time trading rates can differ slightly from the daily reference rate used here.
