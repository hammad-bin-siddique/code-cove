What can I put in a QR code?

Anything text based works — a website link, a WhatsApp number, a WiFi password, a menu link, or plain text like an address. The QR code updates live as you type, so you can preview it before downloading.

Where QR codes are useful

  • Business cards linking to a portfolio or LinkedIn profile
  • Restaurant tables linking to a digital menu
  • Flyers and posters linking to a website or WhatsApp number
  • Product packaging linking to instructions or a warranty page
