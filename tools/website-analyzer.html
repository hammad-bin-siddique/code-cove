What this tool checks

This analyzer fetches the page live, right when you click Analyze, and reports the response time, total page size, and a handful of on page SEO signals: whether the page uses HTTPS, has a title tag, meta description, mobile viewport tag, canonical link, and whether it serves images in WebP format. It is a quick first pass, not a full audit, but it catches the most common issues that slow a site down or hurt search rankings.

A note on real traffic numbers

This tool measures a page's technical health, not visitor counts. Genuine traffic and visitor analytics for any given site require paid data providers or access to that site's own analytics account, so a truly free public traffic checker for any random domain does not exist in an accurate form. If you want to track your own site's real visitors, I recommend enabling Vercel Analytics or Google Analytics directly on your project, both have solid free tiers.
